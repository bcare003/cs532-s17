Bray Wyatt Gets Custom WWE Title Plates (Video), SmackDown Dark Match,
Favorite WWE Couple

   By [22]Marc Middleton | February 14, 2017

   IFRAME: [23]//www.youtube.com/embed/Kea4MDNWfQo?rel=0

   - As seen above, new [24]WWE Champion [25]Bray Wyatt received his
   custom title plates backstage at tonight's SmackDown.

   - The dark match before tonight's [26]WWE SmackDown in Anaheim saw
   [27]Heath Slater and Rhyno defeat [28]Tyler Breeze and Fandango.

   - WWE fans voted [29]Renee Young and WWE Intercontinental Champion
   [30]Dean Ambrose as their favorite company couple in the Valentine's
   social media tournament that wrapped today. They beat [31]Daniel Bryan
   and [32]Brie Bella in the finals.

     Congratulations to [33]@ReneeYoungWWE and [34]@TheDeanAmbrose on
     being voted YOUR favorite [35]@WWE couple in our [36]#ValentinesDay

How Senators Voted on Betsy DeVos

   By THE NEW YORK TIMES FEB. 7, 2017

   The Senate voted to confirm Betsy DeVos as education secretary. For the
   first time in Senate history, Vice President Mike Pence voted to break
   the tie in a confirmation vote. [8]Read our story for more details, or
   view [9]the full list of Senate confirmation votes.

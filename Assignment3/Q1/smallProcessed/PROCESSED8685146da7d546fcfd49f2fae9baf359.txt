   [32]Episode 66: The Technical Wrestling Episode [33]0
   Feb 15, 2017

   Our Royal Rumble Recap went over about as well as Roman Reigns at
   number 30, so this week we're talking technical wrestling! Josh, Kenn,
   Zach and Richard go back and forth on some of their favorite technical
   wrestlers, talk about Kurt Angle vs Chris Benoit at the '03 Rumble, and
   cap the show off with a little bit of Benoit trivia.

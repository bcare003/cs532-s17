[91]WWE SmackDown Live 2/14/2017 Results Coverage

   Posted by Anthony Mango Tuesday, February 14, 2017

   [INS: :INS]

   Tonight's episode of WWE SmackDown for February 14, 2017 is coming to
   you live from the Honda Center in Anaheim, CA. Follow along here for
   ongoing coverage of the results of the show to find out what happens as
   things occur and chat with fellow WWE fans in the comments below!
   Don't forget to keep refreshing this page and checking back to get the
   most up-to-date results coverage!
   [92]spoilers WWE Tuesday Night SmackDown episodes online results
   PROMO: Bray Wyatt heads to the ring. I'm pretty sure he'll say "maaaan"
   and some nonsense about "the darkness" and the planets aligning and
   stuff. John Cena comes out to call attention to how he wants to have
   his rematch right now. AJ Styles decides to come out as well to mention
   how he hasn't had his one-on-one rematch yet and he deserves it before
   Cena. Daniel Bryan makes the judgment call that it will be a triple
   threat match instead.
   TAG TEAM MATCH: American Alpha defeated The Ascension
   On the TitanTron, The Usos show up to say they're gunning for the
   titles.
   BACKSTAGE: James Ellsworth tries to set up Valentine's Day plans with
   Carmella, who wants to stay strictly professional. Dean Ambrose is
   looking for Baron Corbin and bumps into them, offering advice by saying
   he should try a dating website instead. Daniel Bryan comes over to them
   and asks what's going on. Ambrose requests a match with Ellsworth and
   Bryan grants it.
   PROMO: James Ellsworth and Carmella are in the ring. "Don't boo her!
   Carmella's only boo is me." (Lol)
   MATCH: Dean Ambrose vs. James Ellsworth is currently going on and
   should end rather quickly. Oddly enough, quicker than I imagined, as it
   never starts. Instead, Baron Corbin drags Dean Ambrose out as he
   apparently attacked him backstage during the commercial break. Corbin
   and Ambrose continue to fight it out around the stage area. Corbin puts
   Ambrose through a table.
   BACKSTAGE: Daniel Bryan and Nikki Bella talk about how Natalya keeps
   attacking her. Bryan says it needs to stop. Natalya comes over to
   complain. They argue and Natalya eventually throws the first punch,
   starting another fight. They're broken apart and Bryan announces a
   Falls Count Anywhere Match for next week.
   BACKSTAGE INTERVIEW: Dasha Fuentes talks to Baron Corbin about what he
   did a few minutes ago. Corbin says he sent Ambrose to the hospital
   because he robbed him of the WWE Championship at Elimination Chamber.
   INTERVIEW: Renee Young talks to Dolph Ziggler, who says what he did
   this past Sunday was a wake up call to the new generation who can't
   replace him. He's just getting started. Nobody steals his place, but
   some people are about to get put in theirs.
   MATCH: Mickie James defeated Becky Lynch
   PROMO: Naomi is in the ring with Renee Young to talk about her title
   win. She says she has an injury, but she'll work through it. Alexa
   Bliss comes out to trash talk and say that she's giving her one week to
   heal up and face her for the rematch.
   WWE CHAMPIONSHIP TRIPLE THREAT MATCH: Bray Wyatt (c) vs. AJ Styles vs.
   John Cena is about to get started and Luke Harper pops up to attack
   Wyatt during his entrance. The match goes on as scheduled nonetheless.
   Bray Wyatt retains by pinning Cena after a Sister Abigail.
   After the match, Bray celebrates in the ring and Randy Orton comes out.
   He says he refuses to face him at WrestleMania and pledges his undying
   allegiance to him.

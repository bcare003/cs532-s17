Seth Rollins injury update: WWE confirms torn MCL, WrestleMania 33 status ‘in
serious doubt’

   [33]New, [34]4 comments
   by [35]Geno Mrosko[36]@cagesideseats Feb 6, 2017, 9:53pm EST
     * [37]tweet
     * [38]share
     * [39]pin
     * [40]Rec

   It would seem WWE isn’t yet ready to shut the door on Seth Rollins
   working WrestleMania 33 on April 2 in Orlando.

   The company promised an injury update on tonight’s (Feb. 6, 2017)
   episode of Monday Night Raw in Portland and said update was to confirm
   he suffered a torn MCL during Samoa Joe’s attack on last week’s
   episode. They did not, however, say he would missing WrestleMania
   because of it.

   Just that his status for the big show is “in serious doubt.”

   The [41]word from reports online earlier in the day was Rollins had
   flown back to Alabama for another meeting with doctors but WWE did not
   confirm or deny that. Here’s how they played it:

     .[42]@WWERollins tweeted an update about his knee injury. His
     availability for [43]@WrestleMania 33 is still unknown. [44]#RAW
     [45]@SamoaJoe [46]@TripleH [47]pic.twitter.com/WTYsgQPqoB
     — WWE (@WWE) [48]February 7, 2017

   That could mean another trip to Alabama would be to determine if he
   could make it back in time for the show. A torn MCL would make that
   timeline unlikely but wrestlers have come back quicker than one would
   think possible from injuries before.

   We shall see if the same can be said here.

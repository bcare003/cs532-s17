John Cena Celebrates Royal Rumble Win with Make-A-Wish Child

   IFRAME:
   [28]https://www.youtube.com/embed/BNQ1cso9Szs?wmode=transparent&autopla
   y=0&showinfo=0&rel=0&cc_load_policy=0

   [29]Kyle NewportFeatured ColumnistJanuary 30, 2017

   It can be argued that no athlete does as much for fans as John Cena, so
   nobody should be surprised at the way he celebrated his victory at
   Royal Rumble 2017 on Sunday.

   After defeating AJ Styles for the WWE Championship and taking a few
   seconds to soak in the moment, Cena hopped out of the ring and made his
   way into the crowd. It was there that he spent some time with a child
   from the Make-A-Wish Foundation, giving the fan the opportunity to pose
   for photos with the belt (and with the WWE superstar).

   While this interaction would be cool regardless of the situation, it
   was made even better due to its historic nature:

     WWE @WWE

     Congratulations to @JohnCena who has OFFICIALLY tied
     @RicFlairNatrBoy with 16 World Championship victories!
     https://t.co/9voh6glCYo https://t.co/SNnbOXwdLQ
     [30]1/30/2017, 5:02:05 AM

   How many fans can say they were a part of history like this?

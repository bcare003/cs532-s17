WWE Tribute to the Troops 2016 highlights

   United States Champion Roman Reigns teamed up with Big Cass to lock
   horns with WWE Universal Champion KO and The Bulgarian Brute in a match
   with huge Roadblock: End of the Line implications. 12/14/2016 - 22:24

   Three of the greatest trios in WWE history debate who is the greatest
   faction of all time.
   12/14/2016 - 22:00
   In a heated Six-Man Tag Team Match, the No. 1 contender to the WWE
   Title, Dolph Ziggler, joined forces with American Alpha against the
   reigning SmackDown Tag Team Champions. 12/14/2016 - 21:32
   Six Cruiserweight Superstars take a break from WWE 205 Live to honor
   the troops in a fast-paced Six-Man Tag Team action. 12/14/2016 - 21:59
   Apollo Crews answers an open challenge to face The Miz with comedian
   Gabriel "Fluffy" Iglesias in his corner. 12/14/2016 - 21:15

   Cesaro & Sheamus, The Golden Truth, Luke Gallows & Karl Anderson and
   The Shining Stars square off for the right to challenge The New Day at
   Roadblock: End of the Line.
   12/14/2016 - 21:00

   It's safe to say Bayley will not be in the mood to give hugs when she
   takes on Dana Brooke. 12/14/2016 - 21:32

   A confrontation putting United States Champion Roman Reigns and Big
   Cass at odds with WWE Universal Champion Kevin Owens and Rusev leads to
   a huge tag team main event. 12/14/2016 - 21:01

   As part of WWE Tribute to the Troops, Triple H and Stephanie McMahon
   join USO-Metro president and CEO Elaine Rogers in a Wreath Laying
   Ceremony at The Tomb of the Unknown Soldier at Arlington National
   Cemetery.
   12/14/2016 - 22:15

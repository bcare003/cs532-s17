Smackdown Main Event Changed To A Triple Threat Match Tonight

   [109]WWE News
   February 14, 2017
   by [110]Brendan Wahl
   [111]Share7
   [112]Tweet
   [113]Reddit
   Shares 7

   Originally scheduled as Bray Wyatt defending his newly-won WWE Title
   against John Cena, that has now changed in the course of the opening
   segment on Smackdown! Live. After AJ Styles interrupted a Wyatt/Cena
   tet-a-tet, Daniel Bryan inserted him into the title match and made it a
   triple threat contest instead. That will take place in tonight's main
   event.

     Tonight's [114]@WWE Championship Match has become a TRIPLE THREAT
     MATCH!! [115]@WWEBrayWyatt vs. [116]@AJStylesOrg vs.
     [117]@JohnCena!! [118]#SDLive [119]pic.twitter.com/0V0KeqmLLc

     — WWE (@WWE) [120]February 15, 2017

WWE provides an update on Seth Rollins’ injury status

   [32]New, [33]18 comments
   by [34]Sean Rueter[35]@s1rude Feb 3, 2017, 11:40am EST
     * [36]tweet
     * [37]share
     * [38]pin
     * [39]Rec

   After a flurry of news and activity surrounding the knee injury
   suffered by Seth Rollins on Raw this past Monday (Jan. 30) when the
   news first broke a couple days ago, things have been kind of quiet.

   There’s not much of note in WWE’s update to the situation this morning
   (Feb. 3), either. The company announces he’ll miss this weekend’s live
   shows, which anyone who saw [40]the video of his knee bending awkwardly
   beneath him during Samoa Joe’s takedown, or who read [41]the report
   from Wrestling Observer saying he suffered a MCL tear, pretty much
   already assumed.

   That they’ll be providing an update on the next episode of Raw, or that
   they made a point of posting this at all, is another sign they’re
   interested in keeping Seth’s story in our minds - and therefore their
   options for WrestleMania 33, where he was supposed to face Triple H,
   open.

     UPDATE: [42]@WWERollins has NOT been cleared to compete at [43]@WWE
     live events this weekend after re-injuring his knee!
     [44]https://t.co/A9Q8FFrPsE [45]pic.twitter.com/BZMjahwXDT
     — WWE (@WWE) [46]February 3, 2017

     The full extent of Seth Rollins’ injury remains unknown, but
     [47]WWE.com can confirm that the former WWE Champion has not been
     cleared to compete at WWE Live Events this weekend. A full medical
     update will be given this Monday night on Raw.

   Raw should be very interesting, and we’ll have a better picture of
   [48]the Mania card after we get this update on Rollins, and we see
   [49]what Goldberg has to say to [50]Brock Lesnar’s challenge from last
   week.

   Tune in, and stick with us here [51]at Cageside Seats for the latest!

<blockquote><p><i>&#8216;I was on a date with a girl, who was very conservative. <p>Police officers were apparently very unhelpful and the poor guy was left to use a magazine and a bottle of water to remove the matter.</p>
<p>Not content with just brushing the incident off, the guy kept a sample and is now doing his own investigation to find the culprit.</p>

<blockquote><p>&#8216;&#8230;I will plan on ruling you out with DNA for I have kept a sample of the s**t for I can see someone trying to pull another fast one on me,&#8217; he added.</p>
<p>&#8216;And trust me, that is not going to happen. Taking dumps on a person&#8217;s car is something children do, not adults. And although from time to time, I could see why someone deserves it, this was absolutely uncalled for.&#8217;</p></blockquote>
<p>He is even offering â€˜significant compensationâ€™ to anyone who can help him solve the crisis.</p>

<blockquote><p>&#8216;Lastly, a message to the person who did this: I will find you. Do you understand me? I have already had my lawyer draft up subpoena to acquire footage from nearby security cameras amongst other things.</p>

class="body-el-text standard-body-el-text">Deveaux was born out of a dare&ndash;or actually. more like a series of dares. Could a retailer like Breen, who'd previously founded SoHo menswear boutique Carson Street Clothiers (alongside Brian Trunzo, who was an initial partner in Deveaux) make it as a designer? Could a scrappy startup break into the men's luxury market? And could they do all that while also manufacturing exclusively in the United States?</p><p
class="body-el-text standard-body-el-text">"We've faced headwinds from a host of things," Breen admits. For instance, the challenge of finding American manufacturers who could execute exactly what they wanted. "It requires a lot of hand-holding, a lot of back and forth. There's really a lot of steps to that process. We want the quality to be like a lot of European designers who are making things in Italy or Japan. We want to try and push our manufacturers to that point."</p><div

class="body-el-text standard-body-el-text">Three seasons in, it seems like it wasn't such a mistake after all. The day after our conversation, the trio will send a team of models down the Cadillac House runway in a respectably robust assortment of menswear (with a few women's looks introduced into the mix) in front of hi-def drone footage of the Icelandic landscape, one of the collection's key influences.</p><p
class="body-el-text standard-body-el-text">The amount of attention it draws will feel like a major step up for Deveaux, as will the collection itself. <span>It's a little more conservative than spring/summer&ndash;lots of dark tones, Scandinavian-inspired knitwear, and a couple of fairly straightforward suits&ndash;but there's still a sense of play hovering around it, expressed through the architectural lines of a robe coat, speckled trousers, and an unabashedly '70s wide-lapelled shearling coat. Overall it feels like a big step forward into maturity, and one that could easily lure in fans of the past American luxury greats like Calvin Klein that the trio are working hard to emulate.</span></p><div

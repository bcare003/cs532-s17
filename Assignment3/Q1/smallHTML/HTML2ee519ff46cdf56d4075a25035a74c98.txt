					<h3>There’s only one word to describe that match – Phenomenal! ‘The Phenomenal One’ AJ Styles had a brutal war with John Cena at ‘SummerSlam.’ After these two exchanged devastating moves, it was AJ who walked out the winner!</h3>
<p><span id="more-2231138"></span></p>
<p>There was no championship on the line for this match. It was all about pride when <strong>John Cena</strong>, 39, stepped in the ring with<strong> AJ Styles</strong>, 39, on Aug. 21. These two foes have been battling back and forth for months and it came to a head at the Barclays Center in Brooklyn, New York.</p>
<p>This match was amazing – and painful to watch! AJ delivered a suplex on the apron, dropping John’s spine across the edge of the ring. Ouch. Later in the match, AJ countered John’s Attitude Adjustment, hitting a Pele kick before landing the Styles Clash. And John kicked out. Styles returned the favor, kicking out of the AA. AJ even hit an AA of his own!</p>
<p>They pulled out jaw-dropping move after jaw-dropping move, even a top-rope AA. The end of this highly physical match came when AJ his the Phenomenal Forearm for the victory! This match will go down as a modern classic!</p>

<h3><a href="http://hollywoodlife.com/pics/wwe-summerslam-2015-pics/"  target="_blank"  >WWE SummerSlam 2015 — See Highlights From Last Year&#8217;s Event</a></h3>
<p>When AJ made his WWE Debut at the 2016 <em>Royal Rumble</em>, every member of the WWE Universe was asking “When will he face John Cena?” John seemed to answer that question when he made his return to the WWE on May 30, following shoulder surgery. The man dubbed “The Face that Runs the Place” attacked AJ and his fellow members of The Club, <strong>Luke Gallows</strong>, 32, and <strong>Karl Anderson</strong>, 36. This led to a match between John and AJ at WWE’s <em>Money In The Bank</em> on June 20.</p>
<h3 class="shop-title"><a href="http://hollywoodlife.com/shop/how-to-watch-summerslam-online-2016-wwe-network/"  target="_blank"  >WWE &#8216;SummerSlam&#8217;: How To Watch The Hottest Party Of The Summer</a></h3>

During the thrilling climax, Cena hit is finishing move on AJ, <a href="http://hollywoodlife.com/2016/06/20/dean-ambrose-becomes-new-heavyweight-champion-wwe-money-in-the-bank/"  target="_blank"  >the Attitude Adjustment, off of the second rope!</a> It was all just a matter of counting the 1-2-3 after that.</p>
<p>The Club is no longer unified, as <a href="http://hollywoodlife.com/2016/07/19/wwe-draft-live-blog-wrestling-superstars-selected/"  target="_blank"  >Karl and Luke were drafted to <em>RAW</em> </a>while both John and AJ headed over to <em>SmackDown Live!</em> AJ hasn’t needed any backup to torment Cena, as he has mocked the 15-time World Champion, saying that John’s tenure as the icon of the WWE is over. After that impressive victory, it looks like AJ might be right.</p>

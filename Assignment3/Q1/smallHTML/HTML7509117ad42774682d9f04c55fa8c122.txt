These chips ROCK!</p>
<p>I have been a fan of homemade fruit salsa ever since I had the salsa that inspired me to make <a href="http://flippindelicious.com/2015/11/cheese-crisp-tacos-with-pineapple-kiwi-fruit-salsa.html">this pineapple kiwi salsa</a>. I love the crisp bite and fresh sweet flavors. Plus they are super easy to make.</p>
<p>This Chipotle Mango Salsa is no different, and it only has 3 ingredients. It is SO easy to make! All you have to do is dice up some red onion and ripe mango and then toss in a finely chopped chipotle pepper in adobo sauce.</p>
<p><a href="http://flippindelicious.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/chipotle-mango-salsa-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3009" src="http://flippindelicious.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/chipotle-mango-salsa-1.jpg" alt="This Easy Chipotle Mango Salsa is sweet with a hint of heat and it has just 3 ingredients!" width="1000" height="667" srcset="http://flippindelicious.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/chipotle-mango-salsa-1.jpg 1000w, http://flippindelicious.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/chipotle-mango-salsa-1-300x200.jpg 300w, http://flippindelicious.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/chipotle-mango-salsa-1-600x400.jpg 600w, http://flippindelicious.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/chipotle-mango-salsa-1-680x453.jpg 680w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" /></a></p>
<p>A note about canned chipotle peppers&#8230; Sometimes gluten is hiding in there so be sure to double check the labeling and pick a can of chipotle peppers that you feel safe with. I picked a can that didn&#8217;t have<br />
&#8220;spices&#8221; listed in the ingredients. But I have had some cans that did have &#8220;spices&#8221; listed in the ingredients and been just fine. So I am just giving you a heads up to double check your can of chipotles.</p>
<p>Ok, not that I&#8217;ve said that bit, I want to tell you how much I love chipotle peppers! There is so much smoky, spicy, flavor packed into a chipotle pepper! A chipotle pepper is actually a jalepeno that has been smoked. It comes in a little can with several peppers in a smoky sauce called &#8220;adobo.&#8221; Both the peppers and the sauce that they come in really pack a punch of heat and smoke, making them the perfect contrast to the sweet mango in the salsa.</p>

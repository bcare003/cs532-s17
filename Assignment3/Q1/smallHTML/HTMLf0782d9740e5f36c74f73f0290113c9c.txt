When styling Kath, we always want [to highlight] more of her than the dress."</p>

<p>"Trust is very important when it comes to celebrity clients. What we love about our relationship with Kath is that she allows us to take full control of her look for all of her appearances. Fortunately, we know her well enough to know what she likes, how a particular type of dress would fit her, and what would complement her body type and highlight her best features."</p>
